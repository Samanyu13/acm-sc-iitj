iS3: iDeathon on Sustainable Smart Systems

iS3 is the science and technology idea generation event organised by ACM Student Chapter IIT Jodhpur in association with AVL India. It provides a platform for the IIT Jodhpur students to showcase their technical prowess for a sustainable environment. iS3 challenge is the platform for young brains to showcase their ideas and compete with their peers. It is a good opportunity for the IITJ students to rack their brains to provide solutions to different kinds of sectors for removing traditional and digital waste from their processes, organization, products, and services, to radically improve the experience of their ecosystems. The Theme for the event is Sustainable Smart Systems meaning that the participants should frame their Proof of concept (Working Model) which is relevant to Industrial Cost Cutting, Digitization of factories, Quality Control, Energy Saving, Safety & Security, etc. The Concept of IoT and Lean Digital Transformation has the capability to develop smart sustainable systems for the future.

Details

Objective
iDeathon is a fast-paced, intensive brainstorming session that helps people to come up with creative digital transformation solutions to ongoing issues in different sectors. Participants need to explore potential ideas in groups using cutting-edge ideation techniques, including design thinking.

Following are the key areas where participants need to focus and need to develop a working model (PoC) -
  • Smart Home Applications
  • Health Care & Hospitality
  • Smart Cities
  • Agriculture
  • Factory Digitalization
  • Product flow Monitoring
  • Inventory Management
  • Public Safety and Security
  • Quality Control
  • Packaging optimization
  • Logistics and Supply Chain Optimization
  • Energy Saving/Energy Management
  • Smart Grid
  • Buildings & construction

Note: The topics are not just limited to these areas, participants are open to framing their idea to solve any real-life problem.
